Sunday Football & Strip Club Sushi

Jim T. Miller

What's this?

Sunday Football and Strip Club Sushi is the senior show I never had. It's crazy for me to think that I have been writing fiction for four years now and have never put out something for family and friends to read. When people ask me for work, I have no clue what to give them. Now, I'll have this, with pictures!

That's its reason, but what it is I suppose is a different question. Here in Spain, I realized the work I write has a lot to do with home. These stories for me are pizza roll binges and stress-induced insomnia. They're late-night validation-starved Tinder swipes, going full tilt during pick up hoops, and the fever sweat of waking up to Nick at Nite George Lopez reruns. And eating when you're not hungry. And driving for the sake of driving. Oh, America, how I miss you!
