Sunday Football & Stripclub Sushi

Jim T. Miller

What's this?

A mini-collection of my fiction that's several years over my due. The stories within range from ramblings from a few months ago to When I was finishing up my last year at University of Louisville, I had a choice to do an academic thesis or a creative thesis. I decided I would write an academic analysis of a film I loved (No Country for Old Men), which ended up being more about a genre I I hate (Westerns). I never took the chance to collect my creative work and put it together for friends and family to read. Most of the people in my life don't know I write. The ones that do know I write sometimes ask me for work. I either send a 12-point Times New Roman PDF or nothing at all.

I thought it would be nice to change that: do up a little website with a lot of stories and a few doodles for kicks. Sunday Football & Stripclub Sushi is the end result of that desire. This mini-collection is a silly homage to, what's for me, the wildest country on the planet and a type of timeline showcasing my first foray into fiction writing. I hope you enjoy it.
